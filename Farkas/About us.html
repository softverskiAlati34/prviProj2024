Our Mission

At AMETEK, our mission is to provide innovative solutions that enhance the performance of our customers' products and processes.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading provider of advanced technology solutions, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Our History

Founded in 1930, AMETEK has grown from a small manufacturer to a global leader in electronic instruments and electromechanical devices. Our journey is marked by innovation and a commitment to excellence.

Key Achievements

  • 2023: Awarded Best Technology Company by Tech Innovators.
  • 2022: Reached $1 billion in annual revenue.
  • 2021: Launched the EcoSmart product line focused on sustainability.

Product Offerings

AMETEK specializes in a wide range of products including:

  • Electronic instruments
  • Electromechanical devices
  • Advanced sensors and controls

Corporate Social Responsibility

AMETEK is committed to sustainability and community engagement through various initiatives, including:

  • Reducing carbon footprint by 30% by 2025.
  • Supporting local education programs.
  • Implementing recycling programs in all facilities.
