About us:



Samsung is a global leader in technology, delivering innovation across electronics, telecommunications, and home appliances. Established in 1938 in South
Korea, Samsung has grown from a local trading company to a worldwide innovator, creating products that improve the lives of millions every day.

-Our Mission
At Samsung, our mission is to inspire the world and create a better future through transformative products, solutions, and environmental leadership. We are committed to
excellence and a sustainable future for the next generation.


-Our Journey

  • 1938: Founded as a trading company in South Korea.                         
  • 1969: Entered the electronics industry.                                                 
  • 2009: Became the worlds largest technology company by revenue.    
  • 2010: Launched the first Samsung Galaxy smartphone.                      
  • 2020: Released Samsungs first 5G smartphone and foldable devices.


-Our future

Samsung is committed to advancing digital transformation, sustainable solutions, and customer-centric innovations. From AI and IoT to foldable
phones and 5G, we are pushing the boundaries of technology to create smarter, more connected communities.



-Environmental Responsibility

We believe in a future where technology and sustainability go hand in hand. Samsung is dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint through sustainable materials,
green manufacturing processes, and programs that reduce electronic waste.


-Global Presence

With offices and factories in over 70 countries, Samsung is a truly global company. Through local partnerships, educational programs, and philanthropy, we aim to make a positive impact in communities worldwide.
